2015 United States Food Desert Map

About this

I wanted to construct a map that focused on an interesting topic. Though not the most lighthearted, food security is especially important nowadays. The data from the USDA Economic Research Service depicts families and their median income across the United States.

Data Sources

Data obtained through the Economic Reseadch Service (ers.usda.gov); 2015 Census, SF1. Map created by jrwi242, Summer 2020.
